⚡ What is Elektriku Treener?

Elektriku Treener is a free, ad-free, fully offline exam prep app created by an electrician for electricians in Estonia. It helps you confidently prepare for level 3, 4, and 5 qualification exams with realistic simulations, instant feedback, and personalized progress tracking — all without internet.
